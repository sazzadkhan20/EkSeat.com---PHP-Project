EkSeat Ride

EkSeat Ride offers convenient, affordable, and reliable transportation at your fingertips. Whether you're commuting to work, heading to the airport, or going out for the evening, EkSeat has you covered.

Key Features:

  • 24/7 availability in all major cities
  • Multiple vehicle options to suit your needs
  • Real-time tracking and ETAs
  • Cashless and secure payments
  • Share trip details with friends and family

How It Works:

  1. Open the EkSeat app and set your destination
  2. Choose your preferred vehicle type
  3. Confirm your pickup location
  4. Track your driver's arrival in real-time
  5. Enjoy your ride and pay seamlessly through the app

Pricing: Base fare starts at $2.50 + $1.50 per mile. Prices may vary based on demand and vehicle type.

EkSeat Rentals

EkSeat Rentals allows you to book a vehicle for a specific time period, perfect for when you need to make multiple stops or need transportation for several hours.

Key Features:

  • Book by the hour with no per-mile charges
  • Make unlimited stops during your rental period
  • Perfect for errands, shopping, or business meetings
  • Available in 1, 2, 4, and 8-hour blocks
  • Same professional drivers as our regular ride service

Ideal For:

  • Running multiple errands in one trip
  • Business meetings across town
  • Shopping trips where you need to visit multiple stores
  • City tours and sightseeing
  • Any situation where you need a dedicated vehicle for a period of time

Pricing: Starting at $25 for the first hour, with discounted rates for longer rentals.
